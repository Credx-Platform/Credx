FAQ

Common Questions

Is the credit analysis really free?
Yes, 100% free. No credit card required. We analyze your file, identify dispute opportunities, and your specialist calls you — all before you pay a dime.
How long does credit repair take?
Most clients see their first results within 30–45 days. Full results typically come in 3–6 months depending on the complexity of your file.
What does the 90-day money-back guarantee cover?
On the Pro Full Service plan, if we haven't made any verifiable changes to your credit file within 90 days, you get a full refund.
Is my personal information safe?
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted with 256-bit SSL and stored on secure servers. We never sell or share your personal information.
How does the Family Plan work?
The family plan covers up to 5 people in the same household. Each member gets their own analysis and dispute campaign. The base deposit is $150, plus $25/month per additional member. Each member must maintain their own credit monitoring.
